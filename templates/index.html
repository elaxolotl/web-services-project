Efficient Management of Detained Goods and Auctions

Gavel API provides a robust and secure platform for managing detained goods and conducting auctions with ease. Designed for customs officers and buyers, our API ensures seamless integration and efficient handling of goods, bids, and user roles. With our intuitive JSON API, you can effortlessly manage detained goods, place bids, and track auction progress. Whether you're a customs officer looking to add or update detained goods, or a buyer aiming to participate in auctions, our API offers a streamlined experience with clear and accessible data.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Goods Management: Add, update, and delete detained goods with detailed information, including perishable status, expiry dates, and financial details.
  • Secure Auction System: Place bids on goods, view bid history, and manage your bids with role-based access control.
  • User Authentication and Role Management: Secure user signup, login, and role-based access to ensure only authorized users can perform specific actions.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed with real-time updates on goods status, bid values, and auction outcomes.
